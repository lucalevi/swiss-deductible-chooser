About Insurek

What It Does – And Why It Matters

Insurek helps you save money on Swiss health insurance.
By entering your monthly premiums and by knowing your average yearly health costs, it tells you which deductible (franchise) will cost you the least overall. It’s quick, clear, and based on real Swiss insurance data.

Why You Can Trust It

The calculation is simple math – nothing hidden. Insurek compares all six deductible options and shows you where you save the most. The method is public: you can even check the algorithm on GitHub. But honestly, you don’t need to – Insurek does it instantly for you.

Who Made It – And Why

Insurek is a personal project by Luca Iacolettig. When Luca lived in Switzerland, he struggled to choose the right deductible. He built Insurek so others wouldn’t waste time – or money – making the same mistake. You can read more about him on LinkedIn.
