Save on Swiss Health Insurance

Choose the right deductible. Save hundreds every year. Insurek finds the best deductible for you in minutes.

What is Insurek?

Insurek is a personal project by me, Luca. I was frustrated with wasting time trying to find the best deductible in Switzerland, and this is a tool I made to automate the work. You can see the algorithm on Github.

How It Works – 3 Easy Steps

  1. Get your premiums: from the official Swiss Premium Calculator.
  2. Know your yearly health costs: average from the last 3 years.
  3. Enter the data: Insurek shows the deductible that saves you the most.

Step 1 – Get Your Premiums (Priminfo)

Use the Swiss Confederation Premium Calculator (Priminfo) to find the six cheapest monthly premiums in your area.

Go to Priminfo

▶ What to Do on Priminfo:

  1. Enter your ZIP code or city.
  2. Add your birth year.
  3. Choose a deductible (any – it’s just to start).
  4. Select if you need injury insurance (most workers don’t).
  5. At the bottom, choose one or more insurance models (Standard, HMO, etc.).

Then click "Calculate" (berechnen in German). Open the tab "Deductible Comparison" (Vergleich der Franchisen in German) and click on 300 to sort by cheapest premiums. Keep that page open for later.

Step 2 – Your Average Yearly Health Costs

Take your medical bills (doctor visits, medicines, hospital stays – not insurance premiums) from the last 3 years. Add up the amounts of all bills and divide by 3. This average is needed to find the cheapest deductible.

How? By comparing it with the break-even point found in the next step. If your average yearly health costs are below the break-even point, you save money with the highest deductible. And if your costs are above it, the lowest deductible might be better.

Step 3 – Enter Your Premiums

Note: Insurek follows Swiss insurance rules, including the 10% co-insurance limit (max 700 CHF/year for adults). It’s a guide, not financial advice. Check your personal situation before choosing.

Important: Enter the premiums in descending order (highest = 300 CHF, lowest = 2500 CHF).

⚠️ Premiums must go from highest to lowest.

Step 3 – See Your Results

After clicking "Calculate Your Savings", you’ll see:

  • The best deductible for you
  • A clear cost comparison chart

About Insurek

What It Does – And Why It Matters

Insurek helps you save money on Swiss health insurance.
By entering your monthly premiums and by knowing your average yearly health costs, it indicates you which deductible (franchise) will cost the least overall. It’s quick, clear, and based on real Swiss insurance data.

Why You Can Trust It

Insurek uses pure mathematics and Swiss law rules to calculate your optimal deductible:

  • We add up your yearly premiums (your monthly premium × 12)
  • We calculate your out-of-pocket costs (up to your chosen deductible)
  • We include the 10% co-insurance (capped at CHF 700 by law)

The result? A clear comparison showing where you save the most. The math is transparent:

  • Our open-source code is public on GitHub
  • All calculations follow official Swiss health insurance regulations
  • We don't store your data—everything runs in your browser
  • The same math can be used by you, if you do the calculations by hands

Want proof? Check your results against your own calculations. Our recommendations will align because math doesn't lie—it shows you the most cost-effective choice for your situation.
