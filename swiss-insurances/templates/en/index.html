Welcome to the Swiss Health Insurance Calculator

Looking for the best way to save on health insurance in Switzerland? This handy tool helps you find the most cost-effective deductible (franchise) for adults based on your monthly premiums and yearly health expenses. Whether you live in Switzerland or are planning to move, this calculator is your guide to smarter financial choices!

How to Use This Swiss Health Insurance Calculator: Start by visiting the official Swiss Confederation Premium Calculator (available in German, French, and Italian).

Follow these simple steps:

  • Enter your ZIP code or city name in the form.
  • Input your birth year in the designated field.
  • Decide if you need injury insurance (if you work more than 8 hours a week, you don't).
  • Choose a hypothetical deductible to get started.
  • Choose among insurance models like "Standard", "home-doctor", "HMO" or "others" by selecting the relative checkboxes at the bottom.

Next, scroll down and click "calculate" ("berechnen" in German). Once the new page loads, select the "deductible comparison" tab ("Vergleich der Franchisen"). Click the highlighted number "300" to sort premiums from lowest to highest.

You’ll see six different premiums—the cheapest options in your area. Keep this page open, as you’ll these premiums for the next step!

Ready to move forward? Click the button below to enter your premiums.

Get Started

On the next page, input the premiums you found for each deductible (300 CHF, 500 CHF, 1000 CHF, 1500 CHF, 2000 CHF, and 2500 CHF). Here’s what to do:

  • Enter your monthly premium for each deductible option.
  • Click "Calculate" to see your results.
  • Check the chart to understand how costs change with your health expenses.

Info: once you know your yearly average health expenses, you can pick the deductible that saves you the most.

How to Calculate Your Average Health Expenses? Gather all your medical bills from the last 3 years (doctor visits, medications, hospital stays—but not insurance premiums). Add them up and divide by 3 to get your yearly average. This number will help you choose the best deductible!

With this information, you can make a smart decision about your health insurance. Explore more details after clicking the button below.

Get Started

Important Note: This tool uses Swiss insurance rules, including a 10% co-insurance cap of 700 CHF per year for adults, to give you accurate advice. This tool does not take into account all insurance possibilites, but provides just a reference guide for choosing your deductible in a sensible way. Check your needs carefully before choosing a deductible!
