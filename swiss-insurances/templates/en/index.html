Welcome to Insurek, the Swiss Health Insurance Deductible Calculator

Looking for the best way to save on health insurance in Switzerland? This handy tool helps you!

Find the most cost-effective deductible (franchise) for adults based on your monthly premiums and yearly health expenses.

Whether you live in Switzerland or are planning to move, this calculator is your guide to smarter financial choices!

How to Use Insurek

You need two things:

  1. The premium amounts for each deductible (from Priminfo)
  2. Your average yearly health costs (you calculate them)
Then you can use the Insurek tool with this data.

A: Premium Amounts from Priminfo

First of all, find your insurance premiums on Priminfo. You will open the official Premium Calculator of the Swiss Confederation (available in German, French, and Italian).

The orange/yellow button down below brings you to Priminfo.

Once on Priminfo, the official Swiss premium calculator, follow these simple steps:

  1. Enter your ZIP code or city name in the form.
  2. Input your birth year in the designated field.
  3. Choose a hypothetical deductible to get started.
  4. Decide if you need injury insurance (if you work more than 8 hours a week, you don't).
  5. At the bottom of the page, choose among insurance models like "Standard", "home-doctor", "HMO" or "other models" by selecting the relative checkboxes. By default, all models are selected.

Next, still on the official Swiss Premium Calculator, scroll down and click "calculate" ("berechnen" in German). Once the new page loads, select the "deductible comparison" tab ("Vergleich der Franchisen"). Click the highlighted number "300" to sort premiums from lowest to highest.

You’ll see six different premiums—the cheapest options in your area. Keep this page open, as you’ll these premiums for the next steps!

Now go to Priminfo to find your premiums! Click on the orange button below.

Premium Calculator

B: Yearly Average Health Expenses

Now: you will need to know your yearly average health expenses. Once you know that, you can then pick the best deductible that saves you the most thanks to our tool!

How to Calculate Your Average Health Expenses? Gather all your medical bills from the last 3 years (doctor visits, medications, hospital stays—but not insurance premiums). Add them up and divide by 3 to get your yearly average. This number will help you choose the best deductible!
